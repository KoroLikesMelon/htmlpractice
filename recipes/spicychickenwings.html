Have you been craving for some good spicy honey bbq chicken wings?
Well this recipe is absolutely for you,
this is also my personal reccomendation

Ingredients for 20 wings

  • 125 gramms of fluor
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder (or to taste, and optional)
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 teaspoon of paprika (preferrably hungarian)
  • garlic powder to taste, i reccomend a teaspoon, but you could throw this in at the end with fresh garlic in the bbq sauce
  • 20 chicken wings
  • 290 g of bbq sauce
  • 170 g of honey

Step By Step guide

  1. Preheat the oven to around 220 C
  2. Combine the spices(flour chili salt pepper paprika garlic powder), coat the wings evenly
  3. place the wings on parchment paper, dont put them on top of eachother
  4. Bake until golden brown, flip halfwaz
  5. just leave the oven on here
  6. In a separate bowl combine the bbq sauce with honey
  7. Coat the wings in the sauce
  8. Bake for around 10-15 minutes, until the sauce bakes on the wings
  9. Enjoy!
